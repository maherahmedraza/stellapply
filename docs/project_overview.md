## Introduction
StellarApply is an enterprise-grade, AI-powered career automation platform designed to streamline the job application process while ensuring full GDPR compliance. It leverages advanced AI (Google Gemini) to analyze resumes, match jobs, generate tailored cover letters, and track applications.

## Core Value Proposition
- **For Job Seekers:** significant time savings through automated form filling, intelligent job matching, and AI-driven resume optimization.
- **For Enterprises:** Secure, compliant infrastructure (GDPR/DSGVO) with audit logging and identity management.

## Key Features
1.  **AI Resume Builder:** Upload PDF/DOCX; AI extracts and structures data.
2.  **Smart Job Matching:** Vector-based semantic search to find the best career fit.
3.  **Automated Application:** Browser automation to autofill job applications.
4.  **GDPR Compliance:** Built-in consent management, data portability (Article 20), and right to erasure (Article 17).
5.  **Analytics & Tracking:** Real-time insights into application status and funnel metrics.

## Target Audience
- **Professionals:** Seeking efficient job search and application tools.
- **Privacy-Conscious Users:** Demanding full control over their personal data.
